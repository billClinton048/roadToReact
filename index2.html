Uncle Wycliffe’s Resiliency Kit

Tools for thriving through pressure and fear

1. Grounding Tools

  • Box Breathing (Inhale 4s, Hold 4s, Exhale 4s, Hold 4s)
  • Power Pose (2 minutes of strong posture)
  • Speak Slowly Anchor: Tap finger as you say each word
  • 5-4-3-2-1 Senses Countdown
  • Smile intentionally before speaking

2. Reflect & Reframe

Journaling Prompts

  • What does success mean to me, really?
  • What am I afraid will happen if I fail?
  • What’s one small win from my last public talk?
  • What would I say to encourage a friend in my shoes?

Reframing Thoughts

  • "Success is a journey, not a finish line."
  • "The audience wants me to do well."
  • "I’m allowed to grow out loud."

3. Pre-Event Routine

  • Night Before: Review notes, affirm yourself, sleep early
  • Morning Of: Power pose, practice 3 minutes aloud, deep breath
  • Pre-Stage: Smile, slow breath, say mantra: "I’ve got this."

4. Practice & Progress

  • Every Friday: Record a 2-minute talk on any topic
  • Celebrate a win (no matter how small) weekly
  • Ask for feedback once a month

5. Support System

  • Accountability Buddy: [Name/Contact]
  • Inspiration Video: Watch TED Talk
  • Daily Encouragement: “I am not alone in this.”
